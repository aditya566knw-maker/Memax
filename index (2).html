FAQ

Straightforward answers.

What size projects does Memax take on?

We work across the spectrum — from focused 6-week MVPs to multi-year platform builds. Most engagements start between 3 and 6 months.

How do you price engagements?

We work on fixed-scope milestones for well-defined projects and on dedicated-team retainers for longer builds. Every proposal includes a clear cost breakdown.

Do you sign NDAs before the first call?

Yes. We routinely execute mutual NDAs before discovery. Send us your paper or use ours.

Who owns the source code?

You do. All IP, source, and infrastructure credentials are transferred to your organization on delivery.

Which time zones does the team work across?

Our engineers have overlapping working hours with North America, EMEA, and APAC. Every project has a lead in a time zone that matches your team.

What happens after launch?

Most clients continue with a support and iteration retainer. You keep the same core team that built the product.

Have a project in mind?

Tell us what you are building. We will respond within one business day with next steps and a realistic timeline.
